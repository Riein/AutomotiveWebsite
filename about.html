About Paul's Motor Inc

History

Paul is originally from Ukraine but has been living in the United States since 1991. He and his family moved to Vancouver in 1994, where he opened his business. While initially specializing in Jeeps, Paul has worked with Volkswagen, Honda, Toyota, Ford, Chevy, Subaru, Kia, Mazda, and many more car makes. Aside from his business, Paul is active in the Ukrainian Christian community and likes to spend time with his family.
